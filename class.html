About the Instructor

Apirak Tooltham is a lecturer in the Department of Computer & Digital Marketing and Department of Industrial Robots & Automation at Sisongkhram Industrial Technology College, Nakhon Phanom University. He graduated B.Eng. Electrical Engineering from KKU, B.S.in Information Technology from NPU, and M.S. Computer Science from MSU. He is in the process of studying Ph.D. Computer Science at MSU. He teaches courses in Programming, Digital Marketing, Data Science, Senior Project, and he research in the fields of Internet of Things, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science.
